# Sports Digital Network - SEO Strategy Plan

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive SEO strategy for Sports Digital Network (SDN), Canada's premium digital out-of-home (DOOH) advertising network operating in community arenas, recreation facilities, and golf courses. The strategy targets two primary audiences: **advertisers** seeking engaged local audiences, and **venue owners** looking to monetize their facilities.

## Business Context

### What SDN Does
- Operates digital advertising displays in 100+ community sports venues across Canada
- Delivers 35 million weekly impressions to 3.6 million weekly visitors
- Average visitor dwell time: 90+ minutes (vs. 6 seconds for traditional billboards)
- Venues include: multi-pad hockey arenas, golf course clubhouses, recreation centers

### Unique Value Propositions
1. **Captive Audience**: Visitors spend 90+ minutes in venues, not scrolling past ads
2. **Community Context**: Ads appear in trusted, family-friendly environments
3. **Hyperlocal Targeting**: Advertisers can target single venues or go national
4. **Premium Demographics**: Household incomes 35% above national average
5. **Zero Effort for Venues**: SDN handles installation, maintenance, and ad sales

### Business Goals for SEO
- Generate qualified leads from national brand advertisers
- Attract local business advertisers in SDN venue markets
- Recruit new arena and golf course venue partners
- Establish thought leadership in Canadian DOOH space
- Rank for "arena advertising" and "sports venue advertising" terms

## Target Audiences

### Audience 1: National Brand Advertisers

**Profile:**
- Marketing directors and media buyers at national brands
- Agencies managing Canadian media campaigns
- Looking for: scale, premium demographics, brand-safe environments

**Search Behavior:**
- Research-heavy, long consideration cycle
- Compare multiple DOOH options
- Value case studies, ROI data, demographic information

**Key Questions They Ask:**
- "How does arena advertising compare to digital/social?"
- "What demographics attend community arenas?"
- "How do I measure DOOH campaign effectiveness?"
- "What's the reach of sports venue advertising in Canada?"

### Audience 2: Local Business Advertisers

**Profile:**
- Small-to-medium business owners near SDN venues
- Service businesses: dental, legal, automotive, home services, restaurants
- Budget-conscious, ROI-focused

**Search Behavior:**
- Looking for local advertising alternatives
- Searching for specific venue types + advertising
- May not know DOOH terminology

**Key Questions They Ask:**
- "How do I advertise at my local hockey arena?"
- "Is arena advertising worth it for small businesses?"
- "How much does it cost to advertise at a golf course?"
- "What are alternatives to Facebook ads for local businesses?"

### Audience 3: Venue Owners/Operators

**Profile:**
- Arena managers, recreation facility directors, golf course owners
- Municipal recreation departments
- Looking for: passive revenue, facility modernization

**Search Behavior:**
- Searching for revenue diversification
- Looking at what other facilities are doing
- Interested in technology upgrades

**Key Questions They Ask:**
- "How do arenas make money beyond ice rentals?"
- "What is digital signage revenue sharing?"
- "How do golf courses increase revenue?"
- "What's involved in installing arena advertising screens?"

## Technical SEO

### Core Requirements

**Site Speed:**
- Target: <2.5s Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)
- Target: <100ms First Input Delay (FID)
- Target: <0.1 Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS)
- Vercel's edge network handles most optimization
- Use Astro's built-in image optimization

**Mobile-First:**
- All pages mobile-responsive
- Touch-friendly navigation
- Readable text without zooming
- No horizontal scrolling

**Crawlability:**
- XML sitemap at `/sitemap.xml`
- Robots.txt allowing all important pages
- No orphan pages
- Clean URL structure (no parameters)

### Sitemap Configuration

```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<urlset xmlns="http://www.sitemaps.org/schemas/sitemap/0.9">
  <url>
    <loc>https://sdnetwork.ca/</loc>
    <changefreq>weekly</changefreq>
    <priority>1.0</priority>
  </url>
  <url>
    <loc>https://sdnetwork.ca/advertisers/</loc>
    <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
    <priority>0.9</priority>
  </url>
  <url>
    <loc>https://sdnetwork.ca/venues/</loc>
    <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
    <priority>0.9</priority>
  </url>
  <!-- Continue for all pages -->
</urlset>
```

### Robots.txt

```
User-agent: *
Allow: /

Sitemap: https://sdnetwork.ca/sitemap.xml

# Block admin/utility pages if any
Disallow: /api/
Disallow: /_astro/
```

### Canonical URLs

- Every page has self-referencing canonical
- Use absolute URLs: `<link rel="canonical" href="https://sdnetwork.ca/advertisers/">`
- Handle trailing slash consistency (prefer with trailing slash)

### 301 Redirects

If migrating from WordPress, redirect old URLs:
```
/home/ -> /
/about-us/ -> /about/
/for-advertisers/ -> /advertisers/
/for-venues/ -> /venues/
```
